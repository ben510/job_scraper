What We Do

CARMERA’s mission is to improve mobility and city life for all, by maintaining the world’s most robust street intelligence platform.

As the auto industry races towards large-scale production of highly automated vehicles, it depends heavily on globally scalable high definition maps that act like “virtual rails” to ensure safe deployment and a good passenger experience.

CARMERA is solving that need by providing living 3D maps and navigation critical-data to some of the world’s largest and most advanced customers who rely on our data for localization, perception, and path planning reliability.

Who We Are

CARMERA is based in New York City, San Francisco, and Seattle. We are a collaborative, audacious bunch of builders who embrace hard challenges, with a healthy dose of humility and a strong belief in democratizing access to game-changing technology.

We have previously built and scaled groundbreaking 3D, machine learning, computer vision and geospatial technologies at companies like Amazon, Apple, Google and MakerBot to name a few.

If you are invigorated by the chance to be on the front lines of redefining transportation and cities, and making autonomy work for all... Join us.
